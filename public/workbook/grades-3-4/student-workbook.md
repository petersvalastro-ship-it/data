## Unit promise

Your brain helps you **learn**, **remember**, **feel**, **move**, and **make choices**. Screens and games are part of many lives. This unit helps you notice what helps you—and what makes things harder.

**I will learn to:**

- [ ] Talk about what my brain does in kid-friendly ways.  
- [ ] Name a feeling and a calm strategy.  
- [ ] Tell the difference between tech that **helps** and tech that **tricks time**.  
- [ ] Make a simple plan for rest, movement, and creative use of technology.

## E — Technology: tool or trap?

**Tool** = helps you learn, create, or connect in a healthy way.  
**Trap** = makes time disappear or leaves you feeling worse **often**.

Circle **Tool** or **Trap** (you will explain one):

1. Math game with a timer and a clear stop — **Tool / Trap**  
2. Scrolling fast with no break for a very long time — **Tool / Trap**  
3. Making art or music on a tablet for a project — **Tool / Trap**  
4. Watching late when your body needs sleep — **Tool / Trap**
