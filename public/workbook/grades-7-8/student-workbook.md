## 1 — Unit frame

**Essential questions**

1. How do **habits** and **environments** shape attention and learning?  
2. How do **designed systems** (apps, feeds, games) interact with **brain systems**?  
3. What **ethical** responsibilities do creators and users have?

**Architect stance:** You are designing a **life system**—not chasing perfection.

**Contract:** Audits are **non-judgmental**; class is a lab for **evidence** and **revision**.
